Features

Become a more sustainable with simple challenges that allow you reach an optimal carbon footprint by earning some gifts and money in the way.

Stop receiving single-product deliveries and earn points by scheduling your shops correctly.

Reduce your car mileage in a weekly or monthly basis to earn points either using more your bike or planning your commute.
